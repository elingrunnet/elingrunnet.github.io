Transport in Copenhagen

The main transport student use to go the school, is of course their bike. Approximately 70% of the students at Rysensteen bike to school. There are bicycle path all over the city, they are very safe.

In Copenhagen, we have a variety of public transport. All of them are very safe and efficient. Most of the transport are ecologic, as the metro and some buses. On the other side, it is rather expensive, but it is free until you are 12 years old. It is a very alternative to cars that are extremely expensive. In Denmark, the government encourages people to take public transport, to avoid traffic.

They have recently opened a new metro station in Copenhagen to increase the coverage of the transport. Most of the transports are also available over the night, as the metro and all the Abuses. You can combine bicycle and public transport as you can take your bike with you on some trains and metros, exept during rush hours.
